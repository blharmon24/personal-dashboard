How it works

  • Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.
  • Each page canvas is expanded by the note margin width. Odd pages get the margin on your chosen side; even pages get the mirror — so the note space always falls on the outer edge when printed duplex.
  • The binding gutter adds blank space on the spine side of each page, keeping content away from the binding.
  • The page offset lets you shift the odd/even assignment — use 1 if your PDF has a cover page that should not be treated as a content page.
  • Ruled lines are drawn at your chosen spacing and color inside the note margin area.
